This publication investigates the technical and economic feasibility of hydrogen co-firing in Vietnam’s gas power plants over the critical period from 2035 to 2050. It provides insights into decarbonization strategies, energy transition outlooks, and policy impacts for the country’s evolving energy infrastructure.
